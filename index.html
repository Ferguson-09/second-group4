About The Team

We are a group of passionate Software Development students studying at Manitoba Institute of Trades and Technology (MITT). Our journey into software development is driven by curiosity, creativity, and a desire to build technology that solves real-world problems.

As aspiring developers, we are constantly learning new technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern development tools. Through collaboration and hands-on projects, we aim to strengthen our problem-solving skills and prepare for careers in the tech industry.
